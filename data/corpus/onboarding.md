# Onboarding

## Creating your first project
After signing in, choose "New project" from the dashboard. Give it a name and an
optional description. You can invite teammates once the project exists.

## Inviting teammates
Open Project > Members and enter teammate emails. Invitees receive an email and
must accept before they appear as members. Roles can be set during invitation.

## Setting up notifications
Notification preferences live under Settings > Notifications. By default you
receive email for mentions and project invitations only.
