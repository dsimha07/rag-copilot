# Account Policy

## Account lockout
After 5 failed login attempts, an account is locked for 15 minutes. Locked
accounts unlock automatically; there is no need to contact support unless the
lockout repeats.

## Inactive accounts
Accounts with no activity for 24 months are flagged inactive and may be archived
after a warning email. Archived accounts can be restored within 90 days.

## Data access requests
Account owners can request an export of their data under Settings > Privacy.
Exports are prepared within 72 hours.
