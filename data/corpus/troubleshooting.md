# Troubleshooting Guide

## Login problems
If you cannot log in, first confirm your password is correct. If you have
forgotten it, reset it from Settings > Security. Repeated failed logins lock the
account for 15 minutes as a security measure.

## Password reset link not working
Reset links expire 30 minutes after they are sent. An expired link shows an
"invalid or expired token" message. Request a fresh link and use it promptly.

## Two-factor authentication issues
If your authenticator codes are rejected, check that your device clock is set to
automatic. Time drift is the most common cause of rejected codes.
