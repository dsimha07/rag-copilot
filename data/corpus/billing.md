# Billing

## Billing cycles
Subscriptions are billed monthly on the date you first subscribed. Invoices are
emailed to the account owner and are also available under Billing > Invoices.

## Upgrades and downgrades
Upgrades are charged a prorated amount immediately. Downgrades do not refund the
current cycle; the lower price applies from the next cycle.

## Refunds
Refunds are considered within 14 days of a charge. Contact support with the
invoice number to request one.
