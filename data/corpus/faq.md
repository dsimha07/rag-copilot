# Product FAQ

## How do I reset my password?
Go to Settings > Security and choose "Reset password". You will receive a reset
link by email. The link expires after 30 minutes. If it expires, request a new
one from the same screen.

## Why didn't I get the reset email?
Check your spam folder first. Reset emails are sent from no-reply@acme.example.
If nothing arrives within 10 minutes, your account email may be out of date —
contact support to verify it.

## Can I change my plan?
Yes. Plan changes are made under Billing > Plan. Upgrades take effect
immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
